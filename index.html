Short Bio

He is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University. The main research topics are complex system modeling and its applications, evolutionary optimization and learning, time series analysis, and adversarial machine learning

I obtained my Ph.D. from Xidian University in 2020, under the supervision of Jing Liu. I received the B.S. degree from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2015.
